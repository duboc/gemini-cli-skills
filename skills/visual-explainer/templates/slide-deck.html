Platform Modernization

Engineering Strategy — Q2 2026

Architecture Review Board • March 30, 2026

Why Now?

  • Monolith deployment cycle has grown to 4.5 hours — blocking weekly releases
  • Database connection pool saturation at 82% during peak traffic
  • Three critical CVEs in legacy auth module remain unpatched due to coupling
  • New market launch requires independent scaling of payment processing

Target Architecture

Domain-driven decomposition into six bounded contexts, each independently deployable on Cloud Run.

  • Event-driven via Pub/Sub
  • Shared-nothing data stores
  • API Gateway at edge
